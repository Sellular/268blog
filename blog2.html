HTML Buttons

February 23rd, 2020

Although they may seem mundane and simple, buttons are going to be a very important and simple way to interact with a website.

As we continue on in 268, learning javascript and more backend orientated ideas, buttons are going to be a main interaction point between user and website. This video here does a great job just showing the bare basics of buttons and what they can do. Once we learn more about javascript and backend website functionality, having a simple way to trigger a script, query, or change in the website will be very useful and are also abundent in any website available.

<button onclick="window.location.href = 'https://google.com';">GoogleButton</button>
